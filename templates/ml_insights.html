Machine Learning Insights

This page uses retail transaction data to estimate Customer Lifetime Value, identify common product combinations, and detect households at risk of churn.

1. Customer Lifetime Value Prediction

CLV is predicted using a Linear Regression model trained on household spending behavior, including total spend, total units, total baskets, active weeks, and average basket value. Higher predicted CLV indicates households that may generate more long-term revenue.

Model Details

Model Used: Linear Regression

Features: Total Spend, Total Units, Total Baskets, Active Weeks, Average Basket Value

Target: Estimated Future Customer Lifetime Value
